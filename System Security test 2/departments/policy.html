Available Insurance Types

  • Cybersecurity Insurance

    • Covers data breaches and security incidents involving sensitive data like Social Security Numbers (SSN), Taxpayer Identification Numbers (TIN), and financial data. Addresses potential liabilities and costs associated with data breaches and cyberattacks.

  • Financial Liability Insurance

    • Covers financial assets such as credit card numbers and debit card numbers. Protects against financial losses and liabilities resulting from unauthorized use.

  • Business Liability Insurance

    • Covers business data and legal documents. Protects against legal liabilities, document management, and physical records storage.

  • Health Insurance

    • Covers health-related information, medical records, and health insurance policy numbers. Protects against healthcare-related costs and liabilities.

  • Automotive Insurance

    • While not explicitly mentioned, it's inferred from the presence of motor vehicle titles and registrations. Typically covers vehicles and associated liabilities.

  • Travel Insurance

    • Covers travel and immigration-related data. Protects against travel-related incidents and emergencies.

  • Legal Liability Insurance

    • Covers legal case data, immigration data, and legal documents. Protects against legal liabilities, litigation, and compliance issues.

  • Geographic Information Insurance

    • Covers geographic data and indicators. Protects against liabilities related to geographic information and analytics.
